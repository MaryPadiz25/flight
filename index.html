Good to know

Frequently asked questions

Do I need a licence to book a lesson?

No — anyone can create a student account and book a discovery flight with no prior experience.

Can I cancel or reschedule online?

Yes. Cancellations and reschedule requests are submitted through your dashboard and routed to your instructor and admin automatically.

How is bad weather handled?

Admin can mark a lesson "weather cancelled" directly, which notifies you and frees the aircraft and instructor for other bookings.

How do I pay for lessons?

Payments and outstanding balances are tracked in your account; online payment is available directly through the platform.
